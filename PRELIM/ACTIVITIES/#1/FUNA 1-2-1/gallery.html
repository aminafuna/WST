GALLERY BOOK PAGE

Throne of Glass by Sarah Maas

The story follows the journey of Celaena Sardothien, a teenage assassin in a corrupt kingdom with a tyrannical ruler, the King of Adarlan. As the tale progresses, Celaena forms unexpected bonds and uncovers a conspiracy amidst her adventures.

The Selection by Kiera Cass

The story follows a dystopian romance novel where 35 girls are chosen to compete for Prince Maxon's heart in a televised royal competition. Among them is America Singer, who reluctantly participates despite her love for someone else, only to find her life and feelings changing as she navigates the challenges of palace life.

Divergent by Veronica Roth

Is a dystopian novel set in a society divided into five factions, where sixteen-year-old Beatrice "Tris" Prior discovers she is Divergent, meaning she doesn't fit neatly into any one group. As she joins the daring Dauntless faction, Tris uncovers a conspiracy threatening her society and must embrace her uniqueness to fight back.

Frankenstein by Mary Shelley

It tells the story of Victor Frankenstein, a scientist who creates a sentient creature in an unorthodox experiment, only to abandon it out of fear and disgust. The creature, yearning for acceptance but met with rejection, turns vengeful, leading to tragic consequences for both creator and creation.
